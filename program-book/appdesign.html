Design an original app for a specific audience, considering user research, user experience, goals of the audience, and accessibility.

muse is an app designed to aid conscious consumers in their search for ethically-made goods and services. It reccommends mintority-owned, sustainable, and local businesses, as an alternative to corporate consumption. Through a customizable feed and insights about the goals, and practices of each company, muse provides a shopping experience that allows people to support the causes they care about.
